Welcome

Swan Sash Windows is a family-run business specialising in the
supply and installation of traditional timber sash windows and
doors. Since 2009, we have served lots of happy customers in
London, Surrey and the surrounding areas.

Our services range from putting new double-glazed sashes into existing frames,
draught-proofing and box replacements, right through to fixing broken sash cords,
broken springs and replacing panes of glass.

Whether you need new sash windows or your existing windows repaired we can
help you.

What we do

Repairs

We repair cords, springs and box
frames or add new sills, locks and
lifts.

Installations

We can install brand new timber
sash windows into existing box
frames.

Draught Proofing

We can draught proof your existing
period sashes in order to reduce
your heating bills.
